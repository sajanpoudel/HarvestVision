About Us

HarvestVision is a technology-driven company that aims to revolutionize farming methodologies by integrating cutting-edge hardware and software technology. Our initiative, Kissan, uses detailed live data on the temperature, moisture content, and soil nutrition. This project consists of two parts: a farmer component and an expert portion. The farmer first gets onto the website, searches for the soil/land specialist, and then simply schedules an appointment with the chosen expert. And when the expert visits the farmer's field, he or she also carries a gadget that measures the soil's temperature, pressure, and moisture content and its percentage. Using all of these factors, the expert may easily forecast which crops would thrive on a certain soil. The farmer will benefit from this by not suffering a loss and the proper amount of crops can be grown.

Why HarvestVision?

Farmers still grow their plants, seeds, and crops today without the aid of technology or the right understanding of the soil. They only raise the crops in accordance with their needs. When the crops don't develop properly, they employ toxic liquids, medications, and sanitizers. Even the health of the people is being affected, which is an issue for society. What we want to achieve?

We aim to revolutionize the farming industry by utilizing the power of AI to make farming easier and more efficient. Our goal is to provide detailed information and insights to farmers, eliminating the need for human experts. With our advanced technology, farmers will be able to make informed decisions about their crops, soil, and weather conditions, leading to higher yields and less waste. We believe that by bringing together cutting-edge technology and sustainable farming practices, we can create a brighter future for both farmers and society as a whole.
